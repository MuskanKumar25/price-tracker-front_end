Welcome to our website! Here is a little about us.


Our price tracker keeps track of the product that the user wants to purchase at a particular or specific price.
When the desired product's price drops to a specified amount, it notifies the user via the registered email.
Our website helps users to save time as they don't need to manually open the ecommerce website again again and check the drop in it's price every day.
They just need to copy the URL of their desired product and add to our tracker and as soon as it's price drops,
the user gets notified and can buy it before everyone else does or before it gets out of stock!
And here's a plus point to all of this. Users can add unlimited products from the internet for which he/she wants to track the price
and enjoy their online shopping in the best way possible!
